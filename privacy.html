Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-23

Fatha Games ("we", "us") operates mobile applications and games on Android (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our games.

Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through our games.

However, our games may use third-party services (such as advertising and analytics) that can automatically collect certain information for functionality, analytics, and ad delivery.

Third-Party Services

Our games may use:

  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics / Unity Analytics) to understand game performance and improve the experience

These services may collect information such as device identifiers, approximate location (derived from IP), app usage events, crash data, and ad interaction data, depending on your device settings and the service configuration.

Advertising

Ads may be shown in our games. Ad networks may use device identifiers and similar technologies to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can control ad personalization through your device settings and Google Ads settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
