Privacy Policy

Fatha Games operates mobile applications and games on Android. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our games.

Information Collection

We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

In-App Purchases

Our games may offer in-app purchases. All payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information. Google may process transaction-related data according to its own Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services such as Google AdMob and analytics tools. These services may automatically collect device identifiers, usage statistics, crash reports, and diagnostic information to improve performance and deliver advertisements.

Advertising

Advertisements may use mobile advertising identifiers. You can manage ad preferences through your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
